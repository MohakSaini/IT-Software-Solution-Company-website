Networking Services

NWISS provides IT solutions for corporate bodies based on a client/server and peer-to-peer model. NWISS's corporate network solutions create new functional and efficient Local Area Network (LAN) for customers which improve existing organizational workflow.

We ensure that your networks deliver profit for you, by offering services that range from planning and designing a network to its evaluation and health assessment. We understand that networks that are carefully designed and planned reduce capital expenditures and deliver the best return on your investments.
